Manisha Engineers Pvt. Ltd.

Manisha Engineers Pvt. Ltd. is the leading manufacturer of Power & Distribution Transformers with proven expertise spanning over 30 years of excellent work in the field. We offer a unique blend of high-quality products and guaranteed customer satisfaction, earning a reputation for reliability and excellence.

Certified by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) and ISO 9001:2015, we manufacture Power & Distribution transformers ranging from 25 KVA to 10 MVA (11kV, 22kV, 33kV) and single-phase Special Designed Transformers (SDT).

We are a lifetime member of ERDA, Vadodara, Gujarat, where our products undergo rigorous testing at regular intervals to ensure consistent quality. Additionally, we arrange for electrical testing by MSEDCL authorities in the presence of customer representatives to maintain transparency and trust.

At Manisha Engineers Pvt. Ltd., we remain committed to fulfilling customer requirements and enhancing satisfaction by providing high-quality products and services at competitive prices, adhering to quality norms, and ensuring timely delivery.

Our solid infrastructural base includes a robust network of approved suppliers and an established customer base, enabling us to consistently deliver excellence in the field of power and distribution transformers.
